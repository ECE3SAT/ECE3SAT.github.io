Payload

The payload was developed in parallel of the 4 modules: Energy Power Supply (EPS) Telecommunication System (TCS) Attitude Determination Control System (ADCS) OnBoard Computer (OBC) The goal of the payload is to test in practice a physical theory who was validated as a new deorbiting system.The launch is scheduled for the end of 2021.
